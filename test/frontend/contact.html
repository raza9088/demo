Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 5-hour mining session work?

Instead of a 24-hour cycle, PiPortal uses a 5-hour cycle to encourage more frequent engagement. You can start a new session from the dashboard.

What is KYC and why is it required?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is a verification process required by the Pi Network to ensure that all pioneers are real human beings. It's necessary for transferring your mined Pi to the mainnet.

How are referral bonuses calculated?

Referral bonuses increase your mining rate based on the number of active members in your earning team.
